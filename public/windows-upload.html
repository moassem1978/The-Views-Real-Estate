This simplified uploader works on all devices including Windows, iOS, and Android.

Instructions:
  1. The Property ID should be pre-filled from your edit page
  2. Select multiple images to upload (you can select up to 10 images)
  3. Click the "Upload Images" button and wait for confirmation
